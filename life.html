Conway's Game of Life

Welcome to Conway's Game of Life, a fascinating cellular automaton where simple rules create complex patterns. Cells live or die based on their neighbors - too few or too many neighbors cause death, while just the right amount allows survival and reproduction.

Try These Classic Patterns:

  • Pentomino - A small pattern that grows into a larger, stable configuration
  • Pulsar - Creates a mesmerizing oscillating pattern
  • The Eyes - Watch this pattern evolve into something unexpected

Click and drag on the canvas to draw your own pattern, or try one of the classics above!

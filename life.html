Welcome to the Game of Life!

Explore the fascinating world of cellular automata in the Game of Life. Watch as cells come to life, evolve, and form intricate patterns on a two-dimensional grid. Create your own starting point and witness the mesmerizing transformations as the game follows simple rules.

Represented as squares on a canvas, each cell's destiny is determined by its neighbors. Experiment with different initial configurations and see how the game unfolds. If a cell is underpopulated or overpopulated, it dies. On the other hand, a dead cell with exactly three live neighbors comes to life through reproduction.

The canvas awaits your creativity! Dive in and discover the endless possibilities of this captivating cellular automaton.

Try clicking and dragging to create your own starting pattern!
